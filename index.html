About Your Tutor

Ahmed Faioud

I am a dedicated Quran, Arabic and Islamic studies tutor based in Tallinn, Estonia, with years of teaching experience helping students of all ages and backgrounds connect meaningfully with the Quran and the Arabic language.

My approach combines traditional Islamic pedagogical methods with modern, structured learning, so every student progresses with clarity, confidence, and a deep understanding of what they are reciting and learning.

  • PhD researcher in Electrical Engineering, TalTech University, Tallinn
  • Bachelor studies in Arabic Language and Islamic Shariah, Dar Al-Uloom Faculty, Cairo University, Egypt
  • Studied at Arwiqat Al-Azhar, Egypt — one of the world's most prestigious Islamic institutions
  • Specialised in Quran recitation with Tajweed rules
  • Teaching children (ages 5+) and adults, online worldwide and in-person in Tallinn
