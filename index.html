About Your Tutor

Ahmed Bassiouny

What You'll Learn

Programs & Learning Tracks

Structured courses tailored to your level and goals — whether you're a complete beginner or looking to perfect your recitation.

Most Popular
Quran Recitation
Learn to read and recite the Quran correctly from Al-Fatiha through Juz Amma and beyond. Focus on accurate pronunciation, fluency, and connection to meaning.
⏱ 45 min/session 👤 All levels
Tajweed Rules
Master the rules of Tajweed — the science of beautiful Quranic recitation. Covers Noon Sakinah, Meem Sakinah, Madd, Waqf and all core rules with practical application.
⏱ 60 min/session 👤 Intermediate+
Arabic for Beginners
Start from zero — learn the Arabic alphabet, basic vocabulary, and essential sentence structures. Perfect for those wanting to understand Quran and everyday Arabic.
⏱ 60 min/session 👤 Beginners
Advanced Arabic
Deepen your Arabic skills — grammar (Nahw), morphology (Sarf), reading comprehension, and conversational fluency for intermediate and advanced learners.
⏱ 60 min/session 👤 Intermediate+
Kids ♥
Quran for Children
A specially designed curriculum for children (ages 5+) — engaging, patient, and fun. Covers short Surahs, Tafseer for kids, and connection to the meaning of what they memorise.
⏱ 45–60 min 👤 Ages 5+
Islamic Studies
A complete Islamic education track covering Aqeedah (belief), Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence), Islamic Adab (manners & ethics), Seerah (Prophet's biography), and Tafseer — for children and adults.
⏱ 60 min/session 👤 All ages
